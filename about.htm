About us

At Navisco, we are commited to equipping individuals with the necessary skills that will enable them to cope with new technologies and to perform better among their peers. We are committed to building our future leaders in tech.
We have trained over 2000 students from different countries across the globe, majority of which, are Africans.
Our students working together. Photo Credit: @Navisco media

To support our dream, we have made collaborations with top tech industries in countries such as China, Netherlands, Germany, Canada and the USA.
We have helped 1700 of our students get jobs in these industries and we have 300 more under processing.
